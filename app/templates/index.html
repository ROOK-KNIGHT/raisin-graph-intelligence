📈 Graph-Based Prospect Scoring

Scoring Algorithm: We use a weighted graph-based scoring system that combines:
  • Service Diversity: +15 points per service offered
  • High-Demand Industry: +20 points for HVAC, Electrical, Roofing
  • Strategic Location: +15 points for Denver, Atlanta, Chicago
